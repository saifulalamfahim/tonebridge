# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in ToneBridge respectful, safe, and welcoming for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate with empathy and respect.
- Give constructive, specific technical feedback.
- Respect privacy and never publish another person's messages, credentials, or identifying information.
- Use invented, non-sensitive translation examples.
- Accept reasonable maintainer decisions and help keep discussions focused.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, publishing private information, and sustained disruptive behavior are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this policy. Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's available maintainer contact or security-reporting channel. Do not include sensitive details in a public issue.
